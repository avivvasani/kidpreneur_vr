ABOUT US

Kidpreneur is an initiative by **CodeAGS** at the **Ashoka Group of Schools**, designed to ignite the entrepreneurial spirit in young minds. Our mission is to provide a platform where students can learn, explore, and innovate, transforming their creative ideas into tangible projects. We believe that every child has the potential to be a leader and a creator, and Kidpreneur is here to nurture that potential.

This website is a testament to our commitment to hands-on learning. It serves as a digital hub where students can not only discover foundational business concepts but also gain practical experience in thinking like an entrepreneur. From brainstorming innovative ideas to showcasing their projects, we provide the tools and inspiration needed to embark on a journey of enterprise. We are proud to foster a community of aspiring young business leaders who are ready to shape the future.

EXPLORE YOUR PATH

Every great journey begins with a single step. Use these categories to find a business path that sparks your interest.

Technology

Tech-focused businesses are all about innovation. Think about creating a simple app to solve a problem, designing a website for local businesses, or even starting a coding club. The possibilities are endless in the digital world.

Arts & Crafts

If you love to create, this is for you. From selling handmade jewelry and custom art to creating personalized greeting cards, this path lets you turn your artistic talents into a profitable venture.

Services

Sometimes the best business is a service. Consider starting a lawn-mowing service, a dog-walking business, or offering tutoring to younger students. Services are a great way to meet a direct need in your community.

INSPIRATION TIMELINE

Learn from the journeys of history's most inspiring entrepreneurs.

Steve Jobs

Co-founder of Apple Inc. and a pioneer of the personal computer revolution, Jobs was known for his visionary leadership and aesthetic design philosophy.

Oprah Winfrey

A media executive, actress, talk show host, television producer, and philanthropist. She is best known for her talk show, The Oprah Winfrey Show, which was the highest-rated talk show in television history.

Walt Disney

An American entrepreneur, animator, writer, voice actor, and film producer. A pioneer of the American animation industry, he introduced several developments in the production of cartoons.

J.K. Rowling

The author of the Harry Potter fantasy series. She went from being a single mother living on state benefits to one of the world's most successful writers, an example of perseverance.
